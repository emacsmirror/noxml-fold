With noXML fold mode

Note the different folding strategies for elements:

  • q and hi show their content,
  • block elements (p, quote, lg) hide tags and leave content in place
  • the note element gets folded away completely. 

<!-- A fake TEI document -->
[div]
  [p]hello to you![p]
  [p]A quote[p]
  [quote]Another quote[quote]
  [p][quote]Another quote[quote][p]
  [lg]
    [l]verse pada[l]
    [l]and then another[l]⚐
  [lg]
[div]
